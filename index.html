Help & Tips

Data Requirements & File Format

📋 Required Columns

Essential: Tour Name, Tour Manager, Tour Date
Financial: Adult Rate, Senior Rate, Child Rate
Participants: Total Adults, Total Seniors, Total Children
Optional: Optional Name (for add-on services)

📝 Sample CSV Format

Headers:
Tour Name,Tour Manager,Tour Date,Adult Rate,Senior Rate,Child Rate,Total Adults,Total Seniors,Total Children,Optional Name

Sample Row:
QDU,Mayan Hein,2024-10-09,399,0,399,18,0,0,Grand Canyon Heli

💵 Currency & Number Format

Currency symbols ($) and commas are automatically removed. Supports formats like: $399.00, 399, $1,250.50

Dates supported: YYYY-MM-DD, MM/DD/YYYY, DD/MM/YYYY

🔄 Alternative Column Names

System recognizes variations:
• Tour Manager = Manager = TourManager
• Tour Date = Date = TourDate
• Total Adults = Adults = TotalAdults
• Case insensitive header matching

📊 Data Quality Tips

Best Practices:
• Ensure no empty required fields
• Use consistent date formats
• Include all participant counts (use 0 if none)
• Keep manager names consistent across files

⚠️ Common Issues

Upload Failures:
• Missing required columns
• Inconsistent date formats
• Special characters in numbers
• Empty header rows
Check console (F12) for detailed error messages

Revenue Calculation & Core KPIs

🔢 Revenue Formula

Total Revenue = (Adult Rate x Total Adults) + (Senior Rate x Total Seniors) + (Child Rate x Total Children)

Each tour entry is calculated individually, then summed for total revenue. This ensures accurate pricing across all customer segments.

📊 Unique Tours Calculation

Tours are counted uniquely by combining Tour Name + Tour Date. Multiple bookings for the same tour (different participants) count as one unique tour for operational metrics.

💰 Revenue per Participant

Total Revenue divided by Total Participants. Measures pricing efficiency and indicates how well tours monetize each customer. Higher values suggest premium positioning or effective upselling.

👥 Average Group Size

Total Participants divided by Unique Tours. Indicates tour capacity utilization and booking efficiency. Larger groups typically mean better operational efficiency.

🏆 Market Concentration

Revenue share of top 3 managers as % of total. High concentration (>70%) indicates dependence on few performers. Balanced distribution reduces operational risk.

👨‍👩‍👧‍👦 Customer Mix

Percentage breakdown of Adults, Seniors, and Children. Shows primary market segment and helps guide pricing strategies and tour design decisions.

📅 Tour Frequency

Average unique tours per manager. Indicates workload distribution and manager utilization. Helps identify capacity constraints or underutilization.

🎭 Booking Diversity

Average tour types per manager. Higher diversity indicates versatile managers who can handle multiple tour categories, providing scheduling flexibility.

📈 Performance Variance

Coefficient of variation of manager revenues. Lower variance (<30%) indicates consistent team performance. Higher variance suggests uneven skill distribution.

Advanced Analytics Explained

🎯 Performance Rankings

Managers ranked by total revenue, average revenue per tour, tour variety, and customer engagement. Color-coded: 🟢 Excellent (Top 25%), 🔵 Good (50-75%), 🟡 Average (25-50%), 🔴 Needs Improvement (Bottom 25%)

🧠 AI Recommendations

Machine learning analyzes patterns to suggest: Revenue optimization strategies, Tour-manager optimal pairings, Mentorship opportunities, Training priorities, Pricing adjustments

⚡ Efficiency Matrix

Scatter plot showing Tours vs Revenue for each manager. Top-right quadrant = high efficiency. Identifies optimal performers and improvement opportunities.

🎪 Customer Demographics

Pie charts showing Adult/Senior/Child distribution per manager. Helps identify specialization patterns and target market alignment for optimal assignments.

🕸️ Performance Radar

Multi-dimensional view: Revenue Excellence, Tour Diversity, Customer Engagement, Tour Frequency. Shows manager strengths and development areas at a glance.

🔄 Cross-File Analysis

When multiple CSV files are uploaded, the system automatically detects patterns across sources, time periods, and departments for comprehensive insights.
